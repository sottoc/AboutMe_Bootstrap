Frequently Asked Questions

Which plan is best for me?

Looking to have a personalized domain like yourname.me with a professional page to send people? You’ll want the Pro plan. It allows you to add a custom domain or map a domain you already own to your about.me page. Want to be able to capture leads, schedule appointments, or the ability to add photos or videos to your page? Pro is the plan for you. Not ready to make your business or personal brand official? Start out with the free about.me page. You’ll get a simple page and free about.me link, like about.me/yourname. That link will always be yours and you can upgrade later when you’re ready.
